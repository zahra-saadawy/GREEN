ABOUT US

Welcome to GREEN, Your Source for Nature's Treasures Located in the heart of Fayoum, Egypt, GREEN is your gateway to the world of natural olives and aromatic plants. We are passionate about bringing the authentic flavors and fragrances of our fertile region to your table. Pure. Natural. Carefully Made. As a newly established company, we take pride in our commitment to offering only the finest natural products. Each olive and aromatic plant is carefully sourced and cultivated with respect for the land and its people. Our dedication to quality ensures that you receive the purest essence of Fayoum in every bite and aroma.
